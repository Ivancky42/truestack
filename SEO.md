# Truestack SEO Guide

## Implemented Improvements

### 1. Sitemap (`/sitemap.xml`)
- Dynamic sitemap listing all public pages
- Proper priority and changeFrequency for crawler guidance
- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` in production (e.g. `https://truestack.my`)

### 2. Robots.txt (`/robots.txt`)
- Allows all crawlers
- Points to sitemap
- Disallows `/api/` if present

### 3. JSON-LD Organization Schema
- Schema.org Organization markup for Google Knowledge Graph
- Includes: name, description, contact point, logo, area served, expertise
- Validate at validator.schema.org or Google Rich Results Test

### 4. metadataBase
- Ensures absolute URLs for OG images and canonical links
- Uses `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` or falls back to `https://truestack.my`

### 5. Expanded Keywords
- Primary: KPKT license Malaysia, KPKT account management, money lender Malaysia
- Product: e-KYC Malaysia, TrueIdentity, TrueKredit
- Supporting: P2P lending platform Malaysia, PDPA compliant KYC, Lampiran A/B1

## Google Knowledge Panel

Knowledge Panels are automatically generated by Google. You cannot request one directly. They appear when Google has enough signals about your entity.

### What Helps
1. JSON-LD Organization schema (implemented)
2. Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across the web
3. Wikipedia or Wikidata (if notable enough)
4. Official social profiles linked in schema
5. Google Business Profile (for local businesses)
6. Press coverage and backlinks from authoritative sites

### Claiming an Existing Panel
1. Search "Truestack" on Google
2. If a panel appears, click "Claim this knowledge panel"
3. Verify via YouTube, Search Console, Twitter, or Facebook

### Add Social Profiles
When you have LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, add them to the `sameAs` array in `components/seo/organization-schema.tsx`.

## Further Recommendations

### High Impact
- Create a 1200x630 OG image (current favicon is too small for social sharing)
- Submit to Google Search Console and add your sitemap
- Add canonical URLs to key pages
- Consider a blog or resources section for fresh content and long-tail keywords

### Medium Impact
- Product schema for TrueIdentity and TrueKredit
- FAQ schema on product pages
- BreadcrumbList schema
- LocalBusiness schema if you have a physical office

### Ongoing
- Build quality backlinks from fintech publications and industry directories
- Internal linking between services and products
- Monitor page speed with Lighthouse
- Ensure mobile-first experience
